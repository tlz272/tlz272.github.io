Tobias Leigh Robertson is an emerging interdisciplinary artist based in Meanjin (Brisbane), working across writing, video, photography, and sound.

A graduate in Philosophy, History, and Journalism, he approaches making as a way of thinking — shaping forms born of curiosity, improvisation, and quiet discovery. His practice explores the architecture of inner spaces, woven from threads of perception, memory, time, dreams, and consciousness.
