Tobias Leigh Robertson

Tobias Leigh Robertson is an interdisciplinary artist working across film, photography, writing, and music. He is the founder of Vosa Creative, an independent studio for original film and media works. His work has been presented at the Fremantle Biennale (2025), where he co-directed, filmed, edited, and composed sound for a film-poem as part of the Room Service exhibition.

He holds a Bachelor of Journalism and Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy and History from the University of Queensland.

Born in Australia in 2001, Tobias grew up among the lush forests of the Northern Rivers, a landscape that continues to shape his work, visually and sonically. His abiding interest is in the space where creation meets discovery: stepping into the unknown as an explorer, and finding that something is transformed in the process of bringing it back.
